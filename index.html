Places I have lived

  • Denver, Colorado
  • New Orleans, Louisiana
  • Honolulu, Hawaii
  • Orlando, Florida
  • Gainesville, Florida
  • Miami, Florida
  • Coconut Creek, Florida

Denver, Colorado

First settled in Denver when I immigrated to the United States. What I remember most about Denver was the snow. My parents had to shovel snow in the morning to drive to work.

New Orleans, Louisiana

Family moved to New Orleans because dad was an experience fisherman. I remember going to the Mardi Gras parade and collecting beads.

Honolulu, Hawaii

My dad moved to Hawaii for more opportunities in deep sea fishing. Hawaii is known for its beautiful beaches and tourist attractions.

Orlando, Florida

Later on, my family decided to move again to Orlando which is known for its theme parks.

Gainesville, Florida

After a few years, our family moved again to Gainesville which is pretty much a quiet town. It is known for being home to the Univesity of Florida.

Miami, Florida

We later moved to Miami where I attended Florida International University and graduated.

Coconut Creek, Florida

I am now living in Coconut Creek and enjoying this quiet city which is nicknamed the butterfly capital of the world.
